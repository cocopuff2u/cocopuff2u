My Story

I’m a Mac Administrator at NIWC Atlantic with deep experience managing Apple and Linux systems across higher education, government, and high-compliance environments. My work focuses on streamlining workflows, securing endpoints, and advancing open-source tools to support the broader IT community.

Beyond day-to-day operations, I actively contribute to open-source projects and maintain MOFA (Microsoft Overview Feed for Apple)—a platform that provides Mac admins with current software and deployment resources. I’m especially passionate about systems engineering, endpoint security, and cross-platform automation, and I welcome collaboration in these areas.

Outside of work, I enjoy gaming, connecting with my community, and exploring Charleston, SC. I’m also committed to mentorship and sharing knowledge within the Apple admin and open-source spaces.
