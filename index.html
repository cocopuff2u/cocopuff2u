About Me

Hello there! My name is Cody

“Innovation distinguishes between a leader and a follower.”

As a self-taught distinguished Apple Subject Matter Expert (SME) with an impressive 13+ years of experience spanning both the public and private sectors, I have consistently demonstrated excellence in configuring, building, and deploying onsite and remote systems for complex state and international operations. My expertise transcends conventional Apple SME roles, delving into the forefront of cutting-edge technology like Zero Trust Network Access (ZTNA).

Each transition has enriched my knowledge base, enabling me to excel in a wide array of real-world IT challenges.

My dedication to the Apple ecosystem in a predominantly Windows-driven world has fueled my passion for crafting innovative solutions, architectural designs, and systems. My relentless pursuit of knowledge is evident in my ongoing efforts to integrate the Apple ecosystem into non-Apple-centric businesses. This commitment to mastering the intricacies of the Apple environment drives my enthusiasm for continuous innovation.
